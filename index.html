About Us

What is Bull Therapy?

Bull Therapy is a free stock analysis platform designed for everyone — from first-time investors to experienced traders. Our mission is to make professional-grade stock analysis simple, accessible, and actionable.

We combine fundamental data (earnings, valuation, revenue growth) with technical indicators (RSI, moving averages, momentum) and analyst sentiment to generate a clear score: 🔴 Sell / 🟡 Hold / 🟢 Buy.

How It Works

Enter any stock ticker (e.g. AAPL, NVDA, TSLA) and our engine analyzes it across 10 weighted criteria — giving you a score from 0 to 100 and a plain-language verdict.

Every metric includes an explanation so you understand why the score is what it is — not just what it is.

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Bull Therapy ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at bulltherapy.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. Bull Therapy operates entirely in your browser. The following data is stored locally on your device only (localStorage):

  • Search history (stock symbols you have searched)
  • Watchlist (stocks you have saved)
  • Display preferences (theme: light/dark, language: EN/HE)
  • Cached stock data (to reduce API calls, expires after 15 minutes)
  • Optional API keys you choose to enter (stored only on your device)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

3. Third-Party Data Sources

To provide stock analysis, we make requests to the following third-party APIs. Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Yahoo Finance — market price data
  • Finnhub (finnhub.io) — analyst recommendations, trending stocks
  • Financial Modeling Prep (financialmodelingprep.com) — earnings & revenue data
  • CoinGecko (coingecko.com) — cryptocurrency prices
  • CNN Business — Fear & Greed index
  • New York Federal Reserve — interest rate data
  • Alpha Vantage — CPI / inflation data

4. Advertising — Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Bull Therapy uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your preferences, watchlist, and search history locally on your device — this data never leaves your browser. Google AdSense and third-party services may set cookies on your device for advertising and analytics purposes. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, and you may opt out of personalized ads via your consent preferences on this site.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Disclaimer
